Welcome to the home of the UWillDiscover Conference at the University of Windsor!

UWill Discover 2022 will be a 4-day event (March 29th to April 1st) in which undergraduate and graduate students at the University of Windsor can present their research, scholarship, and creative work to their fellow students, friends and family, and the world!

Submissions are now open (Submit proposal via link in tool bar). Also, don't forget to following us on instagram or twitter for more updates!

Browse the contents of UWill Discover Conference:

UWill Discover 2022
The UWill Discover 2022 Conference is planned as a hybrid conference for March 29, 30, 31, and April 1, 2022 (pending health & safety approvals).
