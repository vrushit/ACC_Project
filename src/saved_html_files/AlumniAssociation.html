Enhancing the Lives of Our UWindsor Community

At University of Windsor, we are passionate about adding value to every day life for our alumni and students. In partnership with others and with the active involvement of alumni and friends, we foster pride and the enhancement of the University’s reputation through communication, the celebration of success, and the development of lifelong relationships with graduates, past, present and future.
