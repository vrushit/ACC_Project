Accessibility and Accommodation

The University of Windsor is committed to providing an accessible learning and working environment for persons with disabilities through the identification and elimination of barriers

This includes respect for dignity, independence, integration and equal opportunity as recognized in the University's policies and the Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act, 2005 (AODA).

If you are a student and you would like more information about how the University of Windsor can accommodate your needs on campus, please visit Student Accessibility Services.

Ontario's Accessibility Laws and Standards, via ontario.ca

AODA (Accessiblity for Ontarioans with Disabilities Act) of 2005
