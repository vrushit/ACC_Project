Student Emergency Services

Call 911 in any emergency situation where police, fire or ambulance assistance is required immediately.

UWindsor Campus Community Police

On campus emergencies: Ext. 911
From a cell phone: 519-253-3000 Ext. 4444
Or use any blue light phone on campus

Non-emergency inquiries: 519-253-3000 Ext. 1234
www.uwindsor.ca/campuspolice

My Student Support Program (MySSP)

1-844-451-9700 (North America)
001-416-380-6578 (Outside of North America)
My SSP App (available on the App Store and Google Play)
Access to free confidential counselling 24/7/365 by licensed counsellors, support in over 140+ languages/cultures, mental health information and online resources - www.mystudentsupport.com

Sexual Assault Crisis Centre

519-253-9667 for emergency support
Where expert counsellors can talk to you 24 hours a day - http://www.saccwindsor.net/

Dealing with Sexual Assault and Sexual Misconduct

www.uwindsor.ca/sexual-assault
Contact Dr. Dusty Johnstone, Sexual Misconduct Response and Prevention Office
(519) 253-3000 Ext: 4550 - dustyj@uwindsor.ca

Mental Health Helpline

1-866-531-2600
Free, 24-hour, and confidential health services information - www.mentalhealthhelpline.ca

UWindsor Student Counselling Centre

(519) 253-3000 Ext: 4616
Provides free, confidential, professional counselling - www.uwindsor.ca/scc/
Hours: Monday - Friday - 8:30 am - 4:30 pm.
Closed 12:00 - 1:00 pm

UWSA Peer Support Centre

519-253-3000 ext. 4551
It is a drop-in centre where trained peer support volunteers offer peer counselling - www.uwsa.ca/uwsa-services/peer-support-centre
Hours: Monday – Friday: 10:00am – 8:00pm

UWindsor Care Alert Report

Is a secure online form designed to support you in helping students who exhibit behaviours of concern to get the help they need - https://uwindsor.ca1.qualtrics.com/jfe/form/SV_9Kw8zD8RgexnqW9

You can also provide a Care Alert by contacting a member of the Assessment and Care Team via email at ACT@uwindsor.ca.

Other Campus Resources 

Student Health Services: (519) 253-3000 ext. 7002
Medical services available by appointment only.
Located in CAW Student Centre, 2nd floor, room 242

Student Accessibility Services: (519) 253-3000 ext. 3288
Provides a range of services and supports to students with disabilities.
Located in Dillon Hall, ground floor, room 117
