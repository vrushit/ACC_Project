News Services

News Services is the source for members of the media seeking expert opinion on issues making news of the day.

Journalists can use UWindsor’s News Services to gather information about what’s happening on campus. Students, faculty and staff at the University of Windsor can also consult with members of the News Services’ team for advice and support on how to best tell a story to the public.  

There are many ways to keep up to date with what’s happening at UWindsor:

  • Daily News – An electronic newsletter featuring events and stories about newsmakers at UWindsor
  • Press releases – Announces important advances and significant achievements of the university, its faculty, students, and staff
  • Promote Your Story - You have a great story to tell. Your research and creative activity is important to the University of Windsor and plays a key role in supporting its Strategic Priorities. When you share your stories with the greater community you help the public understand the vital work UWindsor undertakes to make a better world through education, scholarship, research and engagement.

For more information on how News Services can assist your department or unit contact:
