Study in the field

UWindsor offers a wide variety of hands-on learning experiences co-op placements, internships, practicums, exchanges (with 60+ universities in 24 countries!) labs, and community service-learning opportunities. The students abvove went to Iceland to study in the field for course credit!

Learn More

Gain work experience

We have work integrated learning partnerships in place with 250+ companies in Ontario, across Canada, and around the world. More than 93% of UWindsor grads are employed within two years of graduating

Learn More

Change the world

National leaders in providing research opportunities to undergraduate students unlike any other school in Ontario, with over 65% of students who participate. Work with researchers to solve some of the worlds most wicked problems, from curing diseases to climate change.

Learn More

Find your place

A diverse, vibrant & welcoming community. UWindsor is proud to be the home of over 16k students from all over the world, all on the journey of a lifetime. Were the campus where youll fit right in, and we cant wait to meet you.

Learn More

Support Services

Our many support services will help you thrive. Campus community police, dental, mental and health care services, fitness and support centers and multi-faith spaces are just a few of the many ways we will support you through your Lancer journey.

Learn More

Campus Living

Your home away from home. Living in residence means youre not alone when you need help. Staff are on call 24/7 and resident assistants (RAs) are here to help you make friends and become part of the UWindsor community.

Learn More

Student Awards

Your hard work and dedication do not go unnoticed. $14 million in scholarships and bursaries are awarded annually. No matter what program you choose, there could be a scholarship or bursary with your name on it.

Learn More

High-quality Faculty

Here to help you grow, push you, drive you and direct you to new ideas, our award-winning faculty members are one-of-a-kind. Youll get to work with and know your profs within your first year at UWindsor.
